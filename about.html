Born in the vibrant modeling scene of Rome, Katy Lapucci has spent the last five years redefining modern elegance. Her career is a testament to versatility, seamlessly transitioning between high-fashion editorials, commercial campaigns, and runway shows.

Katy's unique look—a blend of classic sophistication and raw, emotive power—has caught the eye of leading photographers and directors worldwide. She believes that modeling is not just about posing, but about storytelling; becoming a silent actor in the narrative of fashion.
